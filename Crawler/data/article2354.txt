DD's Mishra reprimanded over foul language ||||
 Delhi Daredevils (DD) leg-spinner Amit Mishra was reprimanded by the match referee for using inappropriate language during their 27-run win over Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) in the Indian Premier League (IPL) on Saturday. According to the IPL Code of Conduct for Players and Team Officials, Mishra's offense falls under the Level One category of offenses. ||||

Delhi Daredevils leg-spinner Amit Mishra was reprimanded by the match referee for using inappropriate language during their 27-run win over Kolkata Knight Riders in an Indian Premier League (IPL) contest in New Delhi on Saturday. (Fixtures | Squads)
