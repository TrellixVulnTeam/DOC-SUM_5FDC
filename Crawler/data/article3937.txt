'Tamasha' earns 38cr in first weekend in India ||||
 Deepika Padukone and Ranbir Kapoor starrer 'Tamasha' which released on Friday has managed to earn 38.23 crore in India in its opening weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the Imtiaz Ali directorial earned 10.94 crore on Friday, 13.17 crore on Saturday and 14.12 crore on Sunday. Further, the film's worldwide collection has reportedly crossed 50 crore mark. ||||

Deepika Padukone and Ranbir Kapoor starrer ‘Tamasha’s first weekend numbers at the box office are out, and the film has been going head strong. The Imtiaz Ali film has made a total of Rs. 38.23 cr within three days of its release. (Read ‘Tamasha’s review here)
A steady rise has been witnessed in the collections each day at the box office. On the first day of its release, ‘Tamasha’ earned Rs. 10.94 cr, it made Rs. 13.17 cr on the second day and the collections were even higher on day three, which were Rs. 14.12 cr. In all, the film has made Rs. 38.23 cr until now.
Releasing two weeks after Salman Khan’s superhit ‘Prem Ratan Dhan Payo’, ‘Tamasha’ makers seem to have chosen the right slot for its release. Trade Analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted:
#Tamasha has a strong weekend. Fri 10.94 cr, Sat 13.17 cr, Sun 14.12 cr. Total: ₹ 38.23 cr. India biz.

Having brought Deepika and Ranbir back on screen after a gap of two years, Imtiaz Ali film has proved that this is going to be another evergreen couple after Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol which will keep spreading its magic each time they are seen together.
Next big releases of this year are Shah Rukh Khan’s ‘Dilwale’ and Deepika, Ranveer, Priyanka starrer ‘Bajirao Mastani’ which will clash at the box office as both the films will release on December 18.
