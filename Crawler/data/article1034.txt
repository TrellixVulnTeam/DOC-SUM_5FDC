PM Modi wishes Pak PM Nawaz Sharif on Eid ||||
 Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Wednesday called up his Pakistani counterpart Nawaz Sharif to extend his Eid ul-Fitr greetings. Modi also asked Sharif about his health and wished him a speedy recovery as the Pakistani Prime Minister recently underwent heart surgery. While Pakistan is celebrating Eid on Wednesday, India (except J&K and Kerala) will observe the festival on Thursday. ||||

Prime Minister Narendra Modi Wednesday wished his Pakistani counterpart Nawaz Sharif on the occasion of Eid.
In a telephone conversation, PM Modi also enquired about Sharif’s health as he recently returned from London after undergoing a surgery. Previously, the prime minister also wished Sharif a speedy recovery post his operation in London.
