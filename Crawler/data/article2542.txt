Pamela Anderson roped in for 'Baywatch' film ||||
 Actress Pamela Anderson, known for her role as CJ Parker in the original 'Baywatch' TV series will make an appearance in the upcoming 'Baywatch' movie. Actor Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson made this announcement and shared a picture with the 48-year-old actress. Starring Priyanka Chopra and Zac Efron alongside Johnson, the film will release on on May 19, 2017. ||||

Actress Pamela Anderson, who became a household name with her role as CJ Parker on the original “Baywatch” TV series will make an appearance in the movie reboot.
WWE wrestler-turned-actor Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson made the announcement on his Instargam account.
“She introduced the world to ‘CJ Parker’ and became one of the most iconic characters of a generation – for the most successful TV show of all time.
“A pleasure to welcome (as gorgeous as ever) Pamela Anderson to our #Baywatch cast. We couldn’t have made this movie without you. Welcome home. Thrilled you had a blast with us!,” Johnson wrote on his Instagram, along with a photo that appeared to be clicked on the set.
Anderson, 48, played the role of CJ Parker on the original1989 show from 1992 to 1997. However, in the movie, she will not be reprising the same role as model Kelly Rohrbach is portraying Parker.
The movie also stars Zac Efron, Priyanka Chopra, supermodel Charlotte McKinney, Alexandra Daddario, Hannibal Buress and Ilfenesh Hadera as Johnson’s love interest.
The “Baywatch” movie will hit the theatres on May 19, 2017.
