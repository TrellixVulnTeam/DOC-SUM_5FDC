Amitabh Bachchan gets 21 mn followers on Twitter ||||
 Actor Amitabh Bachchan has become the first person in India to have 21 million followers on the micro-blogging site Twitter. He remains the most followed Indian on Twitter ahead of PM Narendra Modi (20.1 million), Shah Rukh Khan (19.6 million) and Salman Khan (17.8 million). Bachchan, who joined Twitter in May 2010 has tweeted over 49,000 times since then. ||||

Megastar Amitabh Bachchan has become the first Bollywood celebrity to have 21 million followers on popular micro-blogging site Twitter.
The 73-year-old megastar beaten his contemporaries Shah Rukh Khan (19.6 million), Salman Khan (17.8 million), Aamir Khan (17.6 million) and Priyanka Chopra (14 million) with his new milestone.


Bachchan’s Twitter account became live in May, 2010 and ever since has been a one stop destination for his fans to connect, interact and engage with the actor.
“#AB21Million … yohohohohohohohohohhhooooooo ..,” he tweeted.

On Twitter, the “Wazir” actor is followed by stars like Mukesh Bhatt, Sridevi, Dilip Kumar, Madhuri Dixit Nene among others. Bachchan has so far made over 49 thousand tweets.
