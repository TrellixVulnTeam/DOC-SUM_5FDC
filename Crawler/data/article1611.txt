Release of 'Junooniyat' postponed to June 24 ||||
 The release date of the Pulkit Samrat and Yami Gautam starrer 'Junooniyat' has been postponed to June 24, which was originally its release date. Earlier, the film's director Vivek Agnihotri had announced that the film would release on June 17. However, the decision to shift the release date again was reportedly made by the film's distributors 'Pooja Films'. ||||

Yami Gautam-Pulkit Samrat starrer Junooniyat has once again been shifted to its original release date.
The intense love story, which was earlier scheduled to hit theatres on Friday (June 17), will now be releasing on June 24, 2016.
The film is directed by Vivek Agnihotri and produced by Super Cassettes Industries Private Limited.
A source close to the production house says, Junooniyat distribution rights has been sold to Pooja Films. It was their decision to pre-pone the release to June 17 and now they have decided to release it on their original release date, June 24. As a distributor, T-series will go by their decision.
