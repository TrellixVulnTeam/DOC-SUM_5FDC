Thatcher voted most influential woman: UK poll ||||
 As per a survey of Britons by Scottish investment company Scottish Widows, former British PM Margaret Thatcher is the most influential woman of the last two centuries. Thatcher, with 28% votes, was ranked ahead of Marie Curie (24%) and Queen Elizabeth II (18%). Princess Diana, Mother Teresa and Oprah Winfrey made it to the top-ten of the list. ||||
