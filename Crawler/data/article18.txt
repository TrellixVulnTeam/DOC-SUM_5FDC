Not making an investment in Netflix: Alibaba ||||
 China's retail giant Alibaba is not making an investment in Netflix, the company's spokesman has confirmed. The spokesman was responding to market rumours about a possible deal between the Chinese e-commerce company and the US video service. Following the rumours, Netflix shares had rose by 3.49% on Friday. ||||

SAN FRANCISCO Alibaba Group Holding Inc is not making an investment in Netflix Inc, Alibaba spokesman Bob Christie told Reuters on Friday.
Christie was responding to market rumours about a possible deal between the Chinese e-commerce company and the U.S. video service.
