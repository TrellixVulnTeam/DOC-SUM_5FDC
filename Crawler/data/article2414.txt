First look of Amitabh, Vidya in 'TE3N' out ||||
 Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh on Thursday took to Twitter to share the first look of actors Amitabh Bachchan, Vidya Balan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui in their upcoming film 'TE3N'. The film has been produced by Sujoy Ghosh and directed by Ribhu Dasgupta. It was earlier scheduled to release on May 20. However, its release was later postponed to June 10. ||||

First look of Amitabh Bachchan, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Vidya Balan’s TE3N is out and from the looks of it the movie looks amazing.
Produced by Sujoy Ghosh and directed by Ribhu Dasgupta, TE3N, which was earlier scheduled to release on May 20, will now hit screens on June 10 this year.
Big B’s movie was clashing with his daughter-in-law Aishwarya Rai Bachchan’s movie Sarbjit.
In the first stills from the movie, we can see Amitabh Bachchan in discussion with Vidya Balan, who is dressed in jeans and kurta.

In some of the stills Amitabh Bachchan is seen riding a scooter and Nawazuddin on a motorcycle.
