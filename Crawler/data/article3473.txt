Arjun Kapoor shares first look of 'Ki and Ka' ||||
 Arjun Kapoor on Friday took to Instagram to share the first look of his upcoming film 'Ki and Ka', also featuring Kareena Kapoor. "Kia & Kabir's romance begins...#kiandka coming soon", he captioned the picture. Directed by R Balki, 'Ki and Ka' is a romantic-comedy which revolves around a married couple played by the duo. It will release on April 1. ||||

The first look of R Balki’s upcoming romantic comedy-drama ‘Ki and Ka’ starring Arjun Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor Khan is a New Year treat to the fans who have been eagerly waiting for the duo to sizzle on screen.
Arjun and Kareena, who will be playing husband and wife in the film, turn on the heat striking a romantic pose holding each other close. The duo is looking extremely comfortable with each other promising a sparkling chemistry onscreen.
Arjun and Kareena will be sharing space for the first time onscreen and are looking great together.
The film, which follows the relationship of the married couples, also features megastar Amitabh Bachchan and wife Jaya in special appearance.
Directed by R Balki, the film is slated to release on April 1, 2016.
