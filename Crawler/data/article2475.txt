Sushma Swaraj admitted to AIIMS ||||
 External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj was on Monday evening admitted to Delhi's All India Institutes of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) after she complained of chest congestion. According to a statement released by AIIMS, "She is under treatment for fever, chest congestion with features of pneumonia. A medical team of specialists is looking after her. Her current condition is stable." ||||

External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj on Monday was admitted to the country’s premier All India Institutes of Medical Sciences (AIIMS).
According to sources at AIIMS, she was admitted to the old private ward of Pulmonary Medicine Department around 5 PM.
