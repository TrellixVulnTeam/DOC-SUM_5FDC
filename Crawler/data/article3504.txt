Kanye West voted GQ's Most Stylish Man of 2015 ||||
 American rapper Kanye West has been voted as the Most Stylish Man of 2015 in a poll conducted by international men's magazine GQ. Kanye, who topped the list for a second consecutive year, was followed by 17-year-old American model Lucky Blue Smith. As per GQ, tweets from Kim, Kourtney and Khloe "spiked the number of votes cast in Wests direction". ||||

Rapper Kanye West has been voted GQ’s Most Stylish Man of the Year for the second year in a row.
The father of one was up against new male model Lucky Blue Smith, who actually just shot a spread for Elle UK with West’s sister-in-law Kylie Jenner.
The magazine explains that while both fan bases came out to help their celeb claim victory, “in the final moments of this year’s showdown, tweets from Kim, Kourtney, Khloe and even Kris Jenner spiked the number of votes cast in West’s direction.”
