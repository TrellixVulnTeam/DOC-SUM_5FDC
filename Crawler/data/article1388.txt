Three-day Summer Festival inaugurated in Delhi ||||
 A three-day Summer Festival, which has a variety of mangoes as the main attraction, was inaugurated by Delhi's Deputy CM Manish Sisodia at Delhi Haat on Friday. The 28th edition of the festival, previously known as the Delhi Mango Festival, also features other summer fruits, beverages, ice creams, cultural and entertainment programmes, besides a demonstration by some chefs. ||||

New Delhi, Jun 24 (PTI) Deputy Chief Minister Manish Sisodia today inaugurated a three-day Summer Festival organised by the Tourism Department of Delhi government at Dilli Haat in Janakpuri here, with mangoes being the main attraction.
Speaking at the event, he said the festival provides visitors not only lots of varieties of mangoes but each and everything that one aspires to eat during summers.
"Besides, this event will also provide an opportunity for farmers to sell their produce and thus promote economic activities within the festival," Sisodia said.
Delhi Tourism has put a large variety of mangoes on display where growers across country rubbed shoulders to showcase the traditional and hybrid varieties, an official said.
Meanwhile, Ola said, it has been appointed as the official travel partner for the festival.
