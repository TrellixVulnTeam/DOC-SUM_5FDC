'Kung Fu Panda 3' top animated grosser in India ||||
 'Kung Fu Panda 3', which released in India on April 1, has become the highest-grossing animated film of all time in India, earning 32 crore, according to Fox Star Studios. To achieve this, the film crossed the 18 crore earnings of 'Ice Age 4'. Directed by Alessandro Carloni and Jennifer Yuh, the film was released across 700 screens in India. ||||

The third instalment in the “Kung Fu Panda” franchise has struck gold at the Indian box office by raking in Rs.32 crore since its release in the country.
According to Fox Star Studios, “Kung Fu Panda 3”, which released in India on April 1, is the highest grossing animated film of all time in India.
“The film has recorded a whopping box office figure of Rs.32 crore in India so far. The film is unanimously cherished by not only kids but also by adults, making it one of the biggest family entertainers. It’s also the highest grosser of all times, making Po one of the most loved characters in India,” read a statement from Fox Star Studios.
