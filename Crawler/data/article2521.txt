Huma Qureshi auditioned for 'The Mummy' reboot ||||
 Actress Huma Qureshi has auditioned for the 2017 reboot of 'The Mummy' franchise featuring actors Tom Cruise and Sofia Boutella, as per Huma's official spokesperson. Huma has reportedly auditioned for the main lead opposite Cruise. Directed by Alex Kurtzman, the new film in the franchise is scheduled to release on June 9, 2017. ||||

Actress Huma Qureshi has auditioned for the third instalment of “The Mummy” franchise featuring Hollywood A-listers Tom Cruise and Sophia Boutella, her official spokersperson has confirmed.
“Huma has recently auditioned for the third part of the successful franchise starring Hollywood star Tom Cruise and Sofia Boutella playing the lead antagonist,” Huma’s spokesperson said in a statement.
If sources are to be believed, Huma has auditioned for the main lead opposite Cruise.


Huma is currently busy shooting for the Hindi remake of the Hollywood film “Oculus”. The film also stars her brother and actor Saqib Saleem in a pivotal role.
