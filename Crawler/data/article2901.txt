Akshay shares picture of son Aarav with PM Modi ||||
 Actor Akshay Kumar on Saturday shared a picture of his son Aarav Bhatia with Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Twitter. The duo met at the International Fleet Review 2016, which took off the Visakhapatnam coast. "Proud moment in a father's life, when the Prime Minister pulls your son's ear in jest & calls him a good boy ;)", Akshay tweeted. ||||
 The Indian Express ||||
 https://full-story.newsinshorts.com/v1/article/27220993-0006-0002-0262-000012345678||||
Prime Minister Narendra Modi was at the International Fleet Review 2016 (IFR 2016) which took off the Visakhapatnam coast on February 6. Also present at the event was Akshay Kumar and Twinkle Khanna’s son Aarav Bhatia. In a picture posted by Akshay on Twitter we see PM Narendra Modi pulling Aarav’s ear. The young star kid is all smiles as he stands with Modi.
Aarav has already earned fame on the social networking sites and this picture is definitely going to be liked by all his fans who follow him on Instagram. After his outing with Saif Ali Khan and the Dubsmashes that have done the rounds in the recent times, this is another add on to his growing fame.
Aarav’s father and superstar Akshay Kumar shared the picture on Twitter and wrote: “Proud moment in a father’s life, when the Prime Minister pulls your son’s ear in jest & calls him a good boy ;).”
