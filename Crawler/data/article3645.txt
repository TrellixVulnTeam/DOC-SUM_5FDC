Hizbul Mujahideen militant arrested in J&K ||||
 A Hizbul Mujahideen militant was arrested after a search operation was launched in the Kupwara district of Jammu and Kashmir, said reports on Saturday. A tip-off led the Special Operation Group and 21 Rashtriya Rifles to conduct an operation in the Khurmorah Rajwara forest. Falak Noor is an 'A' category militant, who had purportedly been active for two years. ||||

A Hizbul Mujahideen (HM) militant has been arrested by security forces from north Kashmir’s Kupwara district, police said on Saturday.
Falak Noor, an ‘A’ category militant, was arrested from a forest area in Handwara area of Kupwara district on Friday, a police official said. Noor was active in north Kashmir area for the past two years, the official added.
