Veteran actress Sadhana passes away at 74 ||||
 Veteran Bollywood actress Sadhana passed away on Friday at the age of 74. She was reportedly suffering from brief illness and breathed her last at Mumbai's Hinduja hospital. Some of her career's hit films were 'Waqt', 'Mera Saaya' and 'Hum Dono'. Sadhana, whose trademark hairstyle 'Sadhana cut' became popular in the 60s, was awarded IIFA Lifetime Achievement Award in 2002. ||||

Sadhana, the actor who reigned over Bollywood as its style icon for many years, passed away in Mumbai on December  25 after a brief illness. The exact nature of her illness is yet to be ascertained. The veteran actress was 74 when she breathed her last at a Mumbai hospital.
The yesteryear’s beauty, who enthralled masses with her graceful performances in Hindi films in the 60s and 70s, had retired from public life long back. The actress only kept in touch with her old pals like Asha Parekh, Helen and Waheeda Rahman. The veteran actresses came to meet the relatives of their late friend Sadhana in the day.
Hiroo Johar, Karan Johar’s mother, also paid visit to the relatives of the late actress during the day time.
Sadhana was known for films like Love in Simla, Parakh, Hum Dono, Waqt, Woh Kaun Thi. She was married to Love in Simla director Ramkrishna Nayyar who passed away in 1995. Sadhana didn’t have any children.
