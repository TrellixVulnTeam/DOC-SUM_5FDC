Sushant to marry girlfriend Ankita in Dec 2016 ||||
 Actor Sushant Singh Rajput and his girlfriend, Ankita Lokhande, on Saturday confirmed that they will be getting married in December 2016. Sushant along with Ankita revealed the date at a media interaction during the Filmfare pre-awards party. The two have been together for over six years as they started dating while they were still working in television shows. ||||

Sushant Singh Rajput and his girlfriend, Ankita Lokhande have finally confirmed that they will be walking down the aisle by the end of December 2016. The duo, who have been dating each other since their television days, looked comfortable sharing their wedding date with the media.
Said Sushant, “Yes we will be getting married in December this year.”
The actor who was flanked by Ankita at the Filmfare pre awards party, also confirmed the date and said that she was looking forward to her wedding.
