'Dilwale' beats 'Bajirao Mastani' at box office ||||
 Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol starrer 'Dilwale' has left behind Ranveer Singh, Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone starrer 'Bajirao Mastani' at the box office in the opening weekend. While 'Dilwale' earned 65.09 crore within three days of its release, 'Bajirao Mastani' grossed 46.77 crore, according to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. ||||
 The Indian Express ||||

‘Bajirao Mastani’ starring Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh had a good run over the weekend and has been unstoppable at the box office.
Deepika and Ranveer’s movie, which had collected Rs. 12.80 crore on the first day, has seen a steady rise in the box office collection thanks to a strong word of mouth.

The movie did a business of Rs 15.52 cr on Saturday (December 19) and on Sunday (December 20), earned Rs. 18.45 cr. Thus the weekend collection of the film is Rs 46.77 cr.
