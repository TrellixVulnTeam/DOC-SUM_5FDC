Kareena, Arjun's 'Ki And Ka' to release in April ||||
 Kareena Kapoor and Arjun Kapoor starrer 'Ki And Ka' will release on April 1, 2016, Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted. The film will also have Amitabh Bachchan and his wife Jaya Bachchan in special appearance, he added. The story of a young, married couple, 'Ki And Ka' is being directed by R Balki. ||||

Director R Balki’s upcoming romantic comedy-drama, starring Kareena Kapoor Khan and Arjun Kapoor, will hit screens on April 1, 2016.
The film follows the story of a young, married couple and also features cameos by megastar Amitabh Bachchan and wife Jaya.
“Eros and R Balki’s #Ki&Ka to release on 1 April 2016. Stars Kareena Kapoor Khan, Arjun Kapoor with Amitabh & Jaya Bachchan in special appearance,” trade analayst Taran Adarsh posted on twitter.
