Astronaut shares photo of frozen Himalayan lake ||||
 American astronaut Scott Kelly has tweeted a photograph of a frozen lake in the Himalayas, which has been identified as the LangaCo Lake off Nepals North-Western border. Kelly is currently on a year-long mission on the International Space Station and had recently also tweeted photographs of South India, which had gone viral. ||||

Like his fellow astronaut Tim Peake, Scott Kelly regularly interacts with Earthians. In fact, his Twitter feed is full of how the universe looks from space. Currently on the year-long mission to the International Space Station, the American astronaut shared a stunning picture of frozen lake in the Himalayas. It has been identified as the La’ngaCo Lake, just across the North-Western border of Nepal.
