Haryana to provide 100% rebate in property tax ||||
 The Haryana government on Thursday decided to provide 100% rebate in property tax for residential, commercial, and institutional properties, said an official release. The rebate would be given for a year from April 1, 2016 to March 31, 2017. The government had earlier waived electricity dues for consumers whose properties were damaged or looted during the Jat quota agitation. ||||

The Haryana government on Thursday decided to provide 100 per cent rebate in property tax for residential, commercial, institutional and other properties.
The move comes as a major relief to all those whose properties were damages during Jat quota stir in February.

A notification to this effect has been issued by the Urban Local Bodies Department, an official release said. The rebate would be give for a period of one year– from April 1, 2016 to March 31, 2017.
Notably, the government has already waived electricity dues for the current and impending billing cycles of all those consumers whose houses or commercial properties were damaged or looted during the agitation from February 15 to 22 in 2016.
