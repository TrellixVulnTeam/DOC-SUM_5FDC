Bajrangi Bhaijaan dominates 2015 Stardust Awards ||||
 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' was named the Best Film of the Year at the 2015 Stardust Awards on Monday. Kabir Khan was named the Best Director of the Year, while Harshali Malhotra won the Breakthrough Performance by a Child Artist for 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'. While Amitabh Bachchan was named the Best Male Actor ('Shamitabh'), Deepika Padukone won the Best Female Actor award ('Piku'). ||||

Salman Khan’s ‘Bjarangi Bhaijaan’ not only did well at the box office this year, but is also bagging most awards at the award functions. The actor’s new avatar as a Hanuman Bhakt had his fans going gaga over him.
Other actors to have made it to the winners list include the likes of Akshay Kumar for ‘Baby’, Amitabh Bachchan for ‘Shamitabh’, Deepika Padukone for ‘Piku’ and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan for ‘Jazbaa’.

Here is the list of winners at Stardust Awards 2016:
Best Film Of The Year : Bajrangi Bhaijaan
Best Actor Of The Year (Male) : Akshay Kumar – Baby; Amitabh Bachchan – Shamitabh
Best Actor Of The Year (Female) : Aishwarya Rai Bachchan – Jazbaa; Deepika Padukone – Piku
Best Director Of The Year : Kabir Khan – Bajrangi Bhaijaan
Best Acting Debut (Male) : Sooraj Pancholi – Hero
Best Acting Debut (Female) : Athiya Shetty – Hero
Best Actor/Actress In A Comic Role : Kartik Aaryan – Pyaar Ka Punchnama 2
Best Child Artist : Harshali Malhotra
Best Playback Singer (Male) : Armaan Malik – ‘Main Hoon Hero Tera’ (Hero)
Best Playback Singer (Female) : Palak Muchhal – ‘Prem Ratan Dhan Payo’ (Prem Ratan Dhan Payo)
Best Lyricist : Varun Grover – ‘Tu Kisi Rail Si’ (Masaan)
Best Music Director : Amit Trivedi – Shaandaar, Bombay Velvet
Best Music Album : Roy – T-Series
Best Choerography : Remo D’Souza – ‘Chunar’ & ‘Vande Mataram’ (ABCD 2)
Style Icon Of The Year Award : Parineeti Chopra
