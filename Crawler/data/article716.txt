J&K: Army kills three terrorists in Poonch ||||
 The Army on Saturday foiled an infiltration bid and gunned down three terrorists along the Line of Control (LoC) in Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch district. The army said the men were Pakistani and possibly belonged to the Lashkar-e-Taiba. The army has cordoned off the area and launched a search operation to track down the remaining terrorists, officials said. ||||

Jammu, July 16 (PTI) Army today foiled an infiltration bid and killed three militants along the Line of Control (LoC) in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir.
A group of militants infiltrated into this side of the LoC. Troops engaged them in Sajiyan area of Poonch district, Defence Spokesman Col S D Goswami said.
