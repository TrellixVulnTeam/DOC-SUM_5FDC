Deepika in 'Hollywood's Next Generation' list ||||
 Actress Deepika Padukone has been featured under the list 'Hollywood's Next Generation' by fashion magazine Vanity Fair. The 30-year old actress can be seen in a red outfit by Italian luxury brand Gucci in the photo shoot for Vanity Fair. Other actors who featured in the list include Elizabeth Debicki from 'The Great Gatsby' and Elvis Presleys granddaughter Riley Keough. ||||

Deepika Padukone is, literally, everywhere. From playing Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s Padmavati to being the femme fatale in Vin Diesel’s xXx: The Return of Xander Cage to now being chosen as Vanity Fair magazine’s Hollywood’s Next Generation, Deepika is winning the world. Oh wait, Deepika is also Bollywood’s highest paid actress now reportedly having got Rs 12 crore cheque for Padmavati (bye bye Kangana Ranaut).
The actress sizzles in a red outfit in Vanity Fair as she is seen exiting a car. For India’s next Hollywood star, Deepika Padukone in that red Gucci outfit is just styled right — sexy, sophisticated and out to win.

Vanity Fair has also included other young actors such as Elizabeth Debicki from The Great Gatsby and The Man From U.N.C.L.E, Lily Collins who played Snow White in Mirror, Mirror, and Elvis Presley’s granddaughter Riley Keough, who was seen in Mad Max: Fury Road.
The magazine also spoke to Deepika during the shoot, producing a behind-the-scenes video. “It’s always comfort over anything else. You need to feel complete, you need to feel like you are wearing the clothes and the clothes aren’t wearing you,” Deepika said about her personal style. And for everyone who wants to know how Indian this star is, here goes: “If I had a choice between a gown and a sari, I’d wear a sari.
