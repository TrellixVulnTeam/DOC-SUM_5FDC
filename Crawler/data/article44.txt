USA wins first gold medal of Rio Olympics 2016 ||||
 USA became the first nation to register a gold medal at the Rio Olympics 2016, after 19-year-old Virginia Thrasher won gold in the 10-metre air rifle on Day One of the event held on Saturday. Thrasher finished ahead of China's Du Li and Yi Siling, who won the silver and bronze medals to open China's medals tally at the event. ||||

The first day of Rio Olympics many things which included the first gold medal winners, a world record broken, a country winning it’s first gold, a country ending a long gold medal drought and many such things.
USA’s Virgina won the first gold medal of the Rio Games in the 10m Air Rifle event while Britain’s Peaty broke the 100. breaststroke world record. Vietnam got its first gold after it won the men’s 10m air pistol final. Also, Australia won a gold medal in swimming, after a long long time.
Katinka Hosszu broke the world record in the 400m individual medley by winning the gold medal in 4 minutes 26:36 seconds.
