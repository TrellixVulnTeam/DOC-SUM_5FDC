Former India cricketer Sachin Tendulkar has undergone a knee surgery at a hospital in London.
Tendulkar posted a picture of his left leg, covered in a black cast, on his official instagram account and commented that he hopes for a speedy recovery.
“Some injuries trouble even after retirement, but I will be back very soon doing the things I enjoy. Had a knee operation & resting,” the iconic batsman wrote.
