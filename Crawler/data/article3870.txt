'Hate Story 3' earns 10 crore on opening day ||||
 Sharman Joshi, Karan Singh Grover, Zareen Khan and Daisy Shah starrer 'Hate Story 3' which released on Friday has earned 9.72 crore. According to trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film is close to recovering its making cost of 13.2 crore, of which 6.7 crore were spent on printing and advertising. "Clearly, the franchise is only strengthening," Adarsh concluded. ||||

‘Hate Story 3’, the third installment of the popular erotic franchise ‘Hate Story’, which released on Friday (December 4), has taken off to a fantastic start on its opening day at the box office. (Hate Story 3 movie review)
‘Hate Story 3’, which was made with a budget of Rs. 13.2 cr, has earned Rs. 9.72 cr on the first day of its release. It has also surpassed the collections of its previous franchises box office collections ‘Hate Story 2’, which had earned Rs. 5.46 cr on its opening day.
Trade analyst Taran Adarsh took to twitter to share the numbers of the movie’s box office collections.
Despite an ordinary star cast, #HateStory3 takes a FANTASTIC start. Fri ₹ 9.72 cr. Should recover costs in opening weekend itself!
— taran adarsh (@taran_adarsh) December 5, 2015
#HateStory3 has cost ₹ 6.5 cr make. Plus, ₹ 6.7 cr for print + advertising. Total cost: ₹ 13.2 cr… SUPER HIT in terms of economics.
— taran adarsh (@taran_adarsh) December 5, 2015
‘Hate Story 3’ opened to 2,690 screens in India which is also comparatively more than that of its previous two installments.
#HateStory: 1078 screens … #HateStory2: 1955 screens … #HateStory3: 2690 screens … Clearly, the franchise is only strengthening!
— taran adarsh (@taran_adarsh) December 4, 2015
‘Hate Story 3’, which stars Zareen Khan, Daisy Shah, Karan Singh Grover and Sharman Joshi, has proved itself in terms of economics and is likely to recover the cost in the opening weekend.
