Smriti Irani faces boycott by Yuva Morcha ||||
 Union HRD Minister Smriti Irani on Sunday faced a boycott by the office-bearers of Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha (BJYM) district unit at the local Circuit House in Jalandhar. They were protesting against the recent appointment of Munish Vij as the unit's district President. The leaders said they would boycott all BJP programmes till the replacement of the district President. ||||

Office-Bearers of the Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha (BJYM) boycotted Union HRD minister Smriti Irani at a meeting in Circuit House on Sunday.
They were protesting the the recent appointment of Manish Vij as the district president of the unit.
Talking to The Indian Express, a senior BJP leader on condition of anonymity, informed that Irani left within 10 minutes as she had to catch a train to New Delhi.
All 48 office bearers said that they would boycott all the BJP programmes till the district president was not changed.
