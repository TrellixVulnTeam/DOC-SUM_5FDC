Alia suffers minor burns due to firecrackers ||||
 Bollywood actress Alia Bhatt on Sunday suffered minor burns on her hands while performing for Big Star Entertainment Awards in Mumbai. As per reports, the accident occurred when excessive firecrackers burst on stage during her performance. The actress was immediately provided with medical assistance and is reportedly recovering at home. ||||

In a shocking incident, actress Alia Bhatt suffered minor burns on her hands at an awards function on Sunday (December 13) night when crackers burst on stage while she was performing.
The freak fire from the crackers ended up scalding Alia Bhatt on her hands. The actress was immediately attended to and given medical aid.
Alia Bhatt soon rushed home in her car and reports are that the chirpy actress is now recuperating at home.
