Big B shares his 'rejected' talent hunt pic ||||
 Bollywood actor Amitabh Bachchan on Monday shared an old photograph of himself on Twitter which he had sent for the Filmfare Madhuri Talent Contest to join films. Posting the black and white picture on the social media platform, Bachchan revealed that the photograph was rejected from the talent hunt. "[I]s it any wonder they rejected me!!", he wrote. ||||

It is every artist’s dream to work with megastar Amitabh Bachchan but before he became the Bollywood’s Shahenshah, Big B like many others was a struggling actor.
Sharing an old picture of his from his struggling days on Twitter, Big B revealed that this was the photograph that led to his rejection from a talent hunt.
