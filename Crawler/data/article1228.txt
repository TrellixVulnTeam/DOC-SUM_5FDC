Xiaomi Mi Max phone launched in India at 14,999 ||||
 Xiaomi on Thursday launched its Mi Max smartphone in India at a starting price of 14,999. The handset runs on Android 6.0 Marshmallow and features a 6.44-inch display, 3 GB RAM, 32 GB memory, 16 MP rear camera and Qualcomm Snapdragon 650 processor. Scheduled for a flash sale on July 6, it will come to stores on July 13. ||||

Xiaomi Mi Max, the large screen smartphone has been launched in India. The smartphone comes with a starting price of Rs 14,999 and runs MIUI 7 based on Android 6.0 Marshmallow.
Xiaomi Mi Max is a large screen smartphone with 6.44-inch display. The display has a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels, translating to a pixel density of 342ppi. Xiaomi Mi Max’s first flash sale is scheduled for July 6 and will be available via open sale from July 13.

Xiaomi Mi Max will come in two processor variants – Qualcomm Snapdragon 650 and Snapdragon 652 SoCs. The 3GB RAM and 32GB internal memory variant is powered by Snapdragon 650 while the 4GB RAM and 128GB storage model get Snapdragon 652 chipset.
Xiaomi has priced the 4GB RAM variant at Rs 19,999. The graphics duty on both the variants is taken care of by Adreno 510 GPU.
Xiaomi Mi Max features a 16MP rear camera with f/2.0 lens and phase detection autofocus. The front camera is a 5MP one with f/2.0 lens.
In spite of its really big display, Xiaomi Mi Max is just 7.5 mm thick and weighs 203 grams. Xiaomi Mi Max is backed by a huge 4850mAh battery.
