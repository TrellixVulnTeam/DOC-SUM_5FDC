Tajikistan experiences 7.2 magnitude earthquake ||||
 Tajikistan on Monday was hit by an earthquake measuring 7.2 on the Richter scale resulting in tremors felt by the subcontinent including North India. The earthquake's  epicentre was 111 km (65 miles) southwest of Karakul. However, no damages were reported. Tajikistan's earthquake comes after a 7. 7 magnitude earthquake rocked northwest Afghanistan in October. ||||

An earthquake measuring 7.2 magnitude struck in Tajikistan on Monday, almost a month a similar quake was felt in North West Afghanistan measuring 7. 7 on the Richter scale.
While the epicentre of the quake was 111 km (65 miles) southwest of Karakul, the tremors were felt across the subcontinent.
