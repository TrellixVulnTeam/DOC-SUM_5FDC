Asus launches ZenFone Max in India at 9,999 ||||
 Asus on Monday launched a new smartphone ZenFone Max at a price of 9,999 in India. The 4G handset runs on Android 5.0 Lollipop and features a 5.5-inch HD display, 13 MP rear camera, 5 MP front camera and 16 GB internal memory. The handset's 5000 mAh battery can deliver up to 38 hours of talk time in one charge. ||||

Asus has introduced the ZenFone Max smartphone in India at price of Rs 9999 which comes with 5000 mAh battery that can also double up as a battery pack to charge other devices.
The pre-bookings for ZenFone Max will open at 12 pm today on Amazon.in and Flipkart.com. It will also be available at ASUS Exclusive Stores and other retailers from the second fortnight of January.
The Asus ZenFone Max’s USP is its battery which offers 37.6 hours of 3G talk time, 32.5 hours of Wi-Fi web browsing.
The Asus Zenfone Max comes with a 5.5-inch HD display and is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 quad-core processor . The smartphone comes with 2GB RAM and 16GB internal memory with an optional expandable memory of 64GB. The rear camera is 13 megapixel while the front camera is 5 megapixel. The Asus Zenfone Max is 4G LTE ready smartphone.
