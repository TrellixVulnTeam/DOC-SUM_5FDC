Former Australian fast bowler Brett Lee will soon be seen promoting his forthcoming romantic comedy film UnIndian on popular Indian comedy TV show, Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hain!
Actress Saumya Tandon who portrays the role of Anita on the show, said, "Lee is one of the most admired cricketers in the world. Glad to hear he is coming on the sets of our show. I have heard that he plays the guitar beautifully, so I am hoping to hear him strum for me."
