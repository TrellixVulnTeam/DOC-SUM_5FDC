BJP announces its UP executive ahead of polls ||||
 The BJP on Tuesday announced its Uttar Pradesh executive ahead of the next year's assembly elections. The executive has 15 vice-presidents, eight general secretaries, and 15 secretaries among others. Notably, Rajasthan Governor Kalyan Singh's son Rajveer Singh Raju was inducted in the executive as a vice-president, while Home Minister Rajnath Singh's son Pankaj Singh was made a general secretary. ||||

Lucknow, July 11 (PTI) Ahead of the crucial Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections slated next year, BJP today announced a jumbo state executive, which include sons of Rajasthan Governor Kalyan Singh and Union Home Minister Rajnath Singh.
The party's state executive has 15 vice-presidents, eight general secretaries, one treasurer, one assistant treasurer and 15 secretaries, a statement issued said here.
"The new executive is a mix of young blood and experienced leadership," a BJP leader said.
Kalyan Singh's son Rajveer Singh Raju and senior party leader Lalji Tandon's son and MLA Gopal Tandon have been included in the list of vice-presidents.
Pankaj Singh, son of Rajnath Singh, has been made the general secretary, while BJP spokesperson Vijay Bahadur Pathak has been elevated as the general secretary.
Walking a tight rope to keep all sections of the party in good humour, state BJP chief Keshav Prasad Maurya retained-newly elected Rajya Sabha member S P Shukla in the executive.
