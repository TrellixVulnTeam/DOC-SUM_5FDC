Indian man dies in theatre watching Conjuring 2 ||||
 A 65-year-old Indian man died while watching the horror film 'Conjuring 2' in a movie theatre in Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu, as per the local police. The man, who was originally from Andhra Pradesh, reportedly complained of chest pain as the film's climax began but passed away before the ambulance arrived. ||||

A man literally could not hold on to his breath while watching the new horror film The Conjuring 2 in a cinema hall in a town in Tamil Nadu on Thursday night.

According to a report in Times of India, the police said that the 65-year-old man died while watching the movie during a night show in a theatre in Tiruvannamalai. He was accompanied by another person. Both were natives of Andhra Pradesh.
When the climax of the film came on screen, he complained of chest pain and went unconscious. He was immediately taken to the Old Government Hospital where he was declared dead by the doctors.
The accompanying person was asked by the paramedics to take the body for postmortem to the Tiruvannamalai Government Medical College Hospital in the outskirts of the town.
However, he reportedly disappeared with the dead body. The police are inquiring lodges in the town, as well as auto drivers, to figure out the identity of the dead person.
