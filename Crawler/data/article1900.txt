SRK hosts dinner for Apple CEO Tim Cook ||||
 Actor Shah Rukh Khan on Wednesday hosted dinner for Apple CEO Tim Cook, who is on his first official visit to India. The dinner, held at SRK's Mumbai residence Mannat, was attended by Amitabh Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Jaya Bachchan, Aamir Khan, Madhuri Dixit and AR Rahman, among others. Reportedly, Cook had earlier expressed his wish to meet SRK. ||||

Dinner hosted by Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan in honour of Apple CEO Tim Cook on Wednesday at his residence ‘Mannat’ turned out to be a starry affair.
Amitabh Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Jaya Bachchan, Aamir Khan, Madhuri Dixit, Farah Khan, A.R. Rahman, Vidhu Vinod Chopra and Sania Mirza, among other celebrities, made their presence at the event.
Cook is in India on his first official visit to the country. (PHOTOS: Shah Rukh Khan hosts party for Apple CEO Tim Cook at Mannat: Aishwarya Rai, Big B, Madhuri are special guests; see pics)


On Wednesday, he announced that the tech giant will establish a design and development accelerator facility in Bengaluru, the home of India’s startup scene.
He also took out time to visit the Shree Siddhivinayak temple in Mumbai. He will also meet Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday during his Delhi visit.
