Three militants killed in Kupwara encounter ||||
 Three militants were killed in an encounter with security forces in the Kupwara district of Jammu and Kashmir on Thursday, the Army said. The bodies of the militants and weapons such as AK-47 rifles have been recovered, an official said. According to reports, two soldiers and eleven militants have been killed in gunfights across the state over the past week. ||||
