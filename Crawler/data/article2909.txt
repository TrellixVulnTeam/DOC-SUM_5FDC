Big B crosses 23 million mark on Facebook ||||
 Amitabh Bachchan on Friday crossed the mark of 23 million followers on social networking website Facebook. "On Facebook 23 MILLION followers!! BAAADUMMBAAA! 23!! THATS 2+3=5...5TH OF FEB...Abhishek's birthday!", he posted on Twitter. Notably, Amitabh is the second most followed Bollywood celebrity on Facebook after actor Salman Khan who has over 29 million followers. ||||

Megastar Amitabh Bachchan, an active user of social networking sites, is thrilled to get 23 million likes on his Facebook page.
The 73-year-old is right behind superstar Salman Khan, whose page has been liked by 29,434,865 fans on the social networking site.
But the “Piku” star, whose page has received 23,032,439 likes, is ahead of superstars Shahrukh Khan and Aamir Khan.
“On Facebook 23 MILLION followers!! BAAADUMMBAAA! 23!! THATS 2+3=5 ..5TH OF FEB ..Abhisheks birthday!,” Amitabh, who was last seen on the big screen in Bejoy Nambiar’s directorial “Wazir”, tweeted on Friday night.
