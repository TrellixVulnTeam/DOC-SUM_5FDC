Baar Baar Dekho charts life story of characters ||||
 Ritesh Sidhwani, the producer of the upcoming film 'Baar Baar Dekho', has said that the film is the life story from 18 to 60 years of two individuals played by Sidharth Malhotra and Katrina Kaif. He added that it is not the standard Bollywood romantic comedy. Reportedly inspired by the film 'About Time', the film will release on September 9. ||||

Excel Entertainment and Dharma Production's Baar Baar Dekho just came out with their first song 'Kala chashma' and as it starts gathering buzz around it, producer Ritesh Sidhwani clarifies the film is anything but the standard Bollywood romantic comedy, that we've seen till date.

Directed by debutante Nithya Mehra, the film follows the story of a two individuals Sidharth Malhotra and Katrina Kaif and their life story from the age of 18 to 60. The film is reportedly based on Richard Curtis's About Time starring Domnhall Gleeson, Rachel McAdams and Bill Nighy.
The Kala Chashma teaser showed us actors like Rajit Kapoor, Ram Kapoor and a bunch of supporting characters. Director Nithya Mehra has previously worked as an assistant director with Ang Lee's Life of Pi and Mira Nair's The Reluctant Fundamentalist.
The film was originally titled Kal Jisne Dekha, but later changed to Baar Baar Dekho. The film is slated to release on September 9.
