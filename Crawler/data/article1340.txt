Jharkhand CM administers oath to plant saplings ||||
 Jharkhand Chief Minister Raghubar Das administered oath to people to save water and plant saplings at the Haryali Sapath Samaroh on Sunday. Asserting the importance of protecting the environment, he also appealed to the people to conserve water and save electricity. This comes ahead of Jharkhand's month-long plantation drive of one crore saplings scheduled to begin on July 1. ||||

Ranchi, Jun 26 (PTI) Jharkhand Chief Minister Raghubar Das today administered the oath to people to 'save water and plant saplings' to protect environment at the 'Haryali Sapath Samaroh', ahead of the month long plantation of one crore saplings scheduled to begin on July 1.
Appealing to save every drop of rain, conserve water and save electricity, Das said environment was a matter for concern and its imbalance was seen clearly.
Union Home Minister Rajnath Singh was present during the programme.
