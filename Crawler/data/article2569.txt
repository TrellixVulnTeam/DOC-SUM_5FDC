First poster of 'Baar Baar Dekho' released ||||
 The first poster of the Sidharth Malhotra and Katrina Kaif-starrer film 'Baar Baar Dekho' was released on Thursday. Directed by Nitya Mehra, the film is based on time travelling, wherein a man gets a chance to see his life ahead and takes decisions accordingly. Also featuring Ram Kapoor, Sarika and Sayani Gupta, it is scheduled to release on September 9. ||||

First poster of Katrina Kaif and Sidharth Malhotra’s Baar Baar Dekho has been released by Dharma Productions and the lead actress is seen sizzling in an orange bikini. Katrina and Sidharth are sharing the frame for the first time.
We also see their pictures from various events in the background, but the pic of them having a merry time on beach seems to take the centerstage.

Katrina was last seen in Fitoor opposite Aditya Roy Kapur, while Sidharth was previously seen with Alia Bhatt in Kapoor and Sons. (Read: Baar Baar Dekho first look: Katrina Kaif, Sidharth Malhotra look nice together)
‘Baar Baar Dekho’, a time travel movie, marks the directorial debut of Nitya Mehra, who has been the assistant director to Farhan Akhtar for ‘Lakshya’ and ‘Don’. The film talks about a man who gets a chance to see his life ahead and then what he does with it. The love story has been extensively shot in Glasgow, Scotland, Krabi in Bangkok and Delhi.
The film will hit the screens on September 9 this year.
