'Wazir' earns 21 crore in opening weekend ||||
 Amitabh Bachchan and Farhan Akhtar-starrer 'Wazir' which hit the screens on Friday, has earned 21.01 crore at the box office across the country in its opening weekend. The Bejoy Nambiar directorial earned 5.61 crore on Friday, 7.16 crore on Saturday and 8.24 crore on Sunday, tweeted Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film has earned 10.48 crore overseas, he added. ||||

Amitabh Bachchan and Farhan Akhtar starrer ‘Wazir’, which released on Friday (January 8) with mixed reviews, has shown an upward trend in its box office collections during the weekend. Due to positive word of mouth, the Bejoy Nambiar directed suspense thriller has collected a total Rs. 21.01 cr. in its opening weekend.
In pics: Amitabh Bachchan, Farhan Akhtar’s Wazir Box Office collections
‘Wazir’, which has been putting up well at the premium plexes of metros, earned Rs. 5.57 cr on its opening day on Friday (January 8), and has shown growth on Saturday and Sunday. The film’s box office business jumped to 28.55% on Saturday (January 9). The film earned Rs. 7.16 cr. on Saturday (January 9) and Rs. 8.24 cr. on Sunday (January 10) which takes the total collection of the film to Rs. 21.01 cr.

The movie also saw a decent growth in the international market as well. There was 55% growth in the USA-Canada, 73% growth in UK and 83% growth in Singapore market and had collected Rs. 7.36 cr. in two days of its release. The total overseas collection of the movie in its opening weekend is Rs. 10.48 cr. till now.

Wazir has passed the weekend test but now is the crucial time for the film when it is in theatres during weekdays.
