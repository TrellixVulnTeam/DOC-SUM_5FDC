Four Jakarta blast suspects arrested ||||
 Indonesia's Police on Thursday detained four suspected militants in connection with the multiple blasts that killed at least six people in the capital city of Jakarta. After six explosions on Thursday, police reportedly killed three attackers in gunfights and arrested four. A bomb disposal unit, police snipers and security officers in armoured vehicles were deployed across the city. ||||
