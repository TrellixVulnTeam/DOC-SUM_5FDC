 Praising the Salman Khan-starrer film 'Sultan', which released today, filmmaker Kabir Khan tweeted, "Sultan delivers a solid punch... A strong Emotional core that really moves you". Salman Khan, Anushka Sharma starrer Sultan releases on Wednesday and fans are making a beeline. While you and me will watch the film today, some lucky Bollywood celebs watched a preview in Mumbai on Tuesday and they can’t stop raving.
From terming it Salman’s best film ever to calling it all heart, the celebs are lavishing compliments on the film, its leads and director. Anushka’s gritty performance as a wrestler also got a lot of applause. We bring you what Bollowood stars such as Karan Johar, Kabir Khan, Huma Qureshi, Dia Mirza and Elli Avaram had to say about the film.
