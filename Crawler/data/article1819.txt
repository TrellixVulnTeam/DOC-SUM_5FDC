Jalandhar, May 21 (PTI) Punjab government will set up a committee to regulate school fees and other charges, a senior BJP leader said today.
BJP leader Manoranjan Kalia said the BJP-SAD state government will constitute a regulatory committee to check the rising fees and other charges in schools across the state.
The decision was taken by Punjab Deputy Chief Minister Sukhbir Singh Badal, who is on a visit to the city, after a meeting over the issue with members and president of parents' association, who were accompanied by Kalia, today.
