Intex launches Aqua GenX smartphone at 13,299 ||||
 Intex on Thursday launched its Aqua GenX smartphone for 13,299. The 4G-enabled phone features a 5.5-inch full HD display, 2GB RAM, a 13MP rear camera and runs on a 1.3GHz Octa-Core processor. The phone has a metallic unibody and a hybrid SIM slot that allows users to insert either two SIM cards or one SIM card and one SD card. ||||

Intex has announced the launch of 4G-enabled Aqua GenX smartphone at Rs 13,299. It features a metal unibody design and 5.5-inch full HD display with Dragon Trail Glass protection.
Aqua GenX is powered by 1.3GHz Octa-Core processor and comes 2GB RAM Mediatek (MT6753) chipset, 16GB ROM, expandable upto 128GB via a microSD card. It comes with 13 mp rear camera with LED flash and a 5 mp front camera.

The smartphone runs on Android OS Lollipop 5.1 and is backed by a 2850mAh Li-Poly battery. It has hybrid SIM slot allowing either 2 SIMs or 1 SIM and a SD Card. It is available in silver and champagne colours.
Aqua GenX packs features like a Q-Key on the right side to set shortcut for any feature, glove mode for touch and my touch to access apps by creating shortcuts on the screen.
