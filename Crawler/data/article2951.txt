Want to watch 'Saala Khadoos': Mike Tyson ||||
 Former professional boxer, Mike Tyson, on Tuesday expressed his wish to watch, the recently released Bollywood film, 'Saala Khadoos', where actor R Madhavan plays a boxing coach. "I'd like to see this boxing film," wrote Tyson on his Facebook page while sharing an article on the film. Notably, 'Saala Khadoos' earned only 6.17 crore, in its opening weekend in India. ||||

R. Madhavan’s ‘Saala Khadoos’ might have not fared well at the box office, but the film has got a special fan. Legendary boxer Mike Tyson took to Facebook to share his desire to watch the “boxing film” ‘Saala Khadoos’.
Madhavan, who had worked for over a year on his physique, was seen with real life boxer Ritika Singh in the film. The actor, who also co-produced the film with director Rajkumar Hirani, shot for the film before ‘Tanu Weds Manu Returns’. The film brought in a new subject and an out of the box story. (Read: Saala Khadoos review: You can pretty much predict what’s going to happen next in Madhavan’s film)
Madhavan plays an aggressive coach shunned by many boxing organizations due to his attitude in ‘Saala Khadoos’. (Also read: Saala Khadoos box office collections: Madhavan’s film earns Rs. 3.37 cr. in two days)
Mike Tyson, who has a record as the youngest boxer to win the WBC, WBA and IBF heavyweight titles at 20 years, is fascinated with the film which has also been made in Tamil titled ‘Irudhi Suttru’. (Read: Saala Khadoos Vs Mastizaade: Watch the audience reaction to Sunny Leone and Madhavan’s film)
He wrote on Facebook, “I’d like to see this boxing film.”

Directed by Sudha Kongara Prasad, Saala Khadoos earned Rs. 9.93 crore in the opening weekend. The film released on January 29 alongside Sunny Leone’s Mastizaade.
