Asin and Rahul Sharma to get married on Jan 23 ||||
 Media reports have confirmed that Bollywood actor Asin and Micromax Co-founder Rahul Sharma will get married on January 23, 2016 in Delhi. The couples save the date invite, which is a golden metal embossed card, is designed by Delhi-based design company EDC which had also designed Harbhajan Singh's wedding card. The couple were introduced by Akshay Kumar in 2012. ||||

Bollywood actress Asin is all set to marry Micromax co-founder Rahul Sharma in Delhi. EDC Designs, that had earlier designed cards for cricketer Harbhajan Singh and Geeta Basra’s wedding, has been mantle of designing the cards for Asin and Rahul’s wedding.
“The ‘Save The Date’ is a metal embossed card done for a select guest list. Both Asin and Rahul were involved in choosing the design and the overall look of the card. The ‘Save The Date’ invite will be followed by a party card,” said a source. (See Pics: Asin to marry Micromax co-founder Rahul Sharma)
While the wedding will take place in Delhi, Asin is expected to throw a party in Mumbai for her Bollywood friends.
Asin, who was last seen onscreen in ‘All Is Well’, has wrapped all her Bollywood assignments and taking a sabbatical from movies.
