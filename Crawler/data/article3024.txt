Excel Entertainment announces 'Fukrey' sequel ||||
 A sequel to the 2013 comedy film 'Fukrey' has been announced by producers Excel Entertainment. The film, which will be shot in Delhi, will retain its original leading cast, namely Pulkit Samrat, Varun Sharma, Manjot Singh, Ali Fazal and Richa Chadda. The sequel will be directed by Mrigdeep Singh Lamba and will go on floors in August 2016. ||||
 The Indian Express ||||

Richa Chadha is all set to entertain her fans as Bholi Punjaban once again in the sequel of Fukrey. Yes! That’s true. The makers of the film have finally decided upon the sequel as well carrying forward the story with the same cast from the original movie.
The cast will include Pulkit Samrat, Varun Sharma, Manjot Singh, Ali Fazal and Richa Chadha. The sequel, which will be shot in Delhi, is being produced by Excel Entertainment and directed by Mrighdeep Singh Lamba. It will go on floors in August this year. The announcement of the film was made on Twitter by Taran Adarsh. He tweeted:
#Fukrey sequel announced. Will have the original cast: Pulkit Samrat, Varun Sharma, Manjot Singh, Ali Fazal and Richa Chadha… contd.
— taran adarsh (@taran_adarsh) January 30, 2016
#Fukrey sequel will be produced by Excel Entertainment and directed by Mrighdeep Singh Lamba. Shooting begins in August in Delhi.

Fukrey, which was also directed by Mrigdeep Singh Lamba released in 2013 and earned a total of Rs. 36.50 cr during its entire run. The film, which was liked by many, also helped Richa Chadha earn recognition in the industry.
