Salman plants saplings in tree plantation drive ||||
 Actor Salman Khan on Sunday planted tree saplings in Mumbai while appreciating the Brihan Mumbai Municipal Corporation for its decision to plant two crore saplings across Maharashtra. The 50-year-old actor tweeted pictures from the tree plantation drive where he planted saplings alongside the public. Notably, he runs the Being Human Foundation, a charitable organisation for the underprivileged. ||||
