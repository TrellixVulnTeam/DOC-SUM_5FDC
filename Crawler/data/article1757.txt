AAP to fight Goa Assembly polls: Kejriwal ||||
 Delhi CM and AAP chief Arvind Kejriwal on Sunday announced that the party will fight the Goa Assembly elections, to be held in 2017. Kejriwal also alleged that BJP and Congress have tarnished the image of Goa tourism and AAP will try to 'cleanse' it. AAP is also contesting in Punjab which will go for elections alongside Goa next year. ||||

New Delhi, May 22 (PTI) Aam Aadmi Party will fight the Goa Assembly polls scheduled next year, Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal today said.
The party has for the first time officially announced its plans of contesting elections in Goa which will go to polls along with Punjab.
"AAP will fight elections in Goa," Kejriwal, the national convenor of AAP, tweeted.
The party has been mobilising its resources in the state to take on the ruling BJP and Congress.
