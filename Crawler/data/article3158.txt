Lava launches P7 smartphone at 5,499 in India ||||
 Lava on Thursday launched a new smartphone P7 at 5,499 in India. The new 3G-enabled dual-sim handset features a 5-inch display, 5 MP rear camera, 2 MP front camera and 8 GB of expandable internal storage space. The Lava P7 runs on Android 5.1 Lollipop operating system and comes with a guaranteed upgrade to Android 6.0 Marshmallow. ||||

Lava has launched another entry level smartphone P7 in India. The smartphone runs Android 5.1 Lollipop with guaranteed upgrade to Android 6.0 Marshmallow.
The Lava P7 features a 5-inch display with a resolution of 854 x 480 pixels and is powered by a 32bit quad-core MediaTek processor clocked at 1.2GHz. The dual-SIM smartphone comes with 1GB RAM and 8GB expandable storage.
The Lava P7 smartphone sports a 5-megapixel rear camera and 2-megapixel front camera with LED flash. Lava has also customised the camera software with features like Live Photo, Face Beauty and Voice capture.
The Lava P7 is a 3G smartphone and comes with 2000mAh battery. On the connectivity front, Lava P7 supports Wi-Fiu, Bluetooth and GPS.
The Lava P7 smartphone will be available in White, Blue and Gold colour options. The smartphone is priced at Rs 5,499 and will be available via online and offline retail channels.
