Salman Khan receives death threat call ||||
 Bollywood actor Salman Khan recently received a threat phone call from an anonymous person, an official informed today. The Mumbai police have launched a probe into the matter. According to the police, they have located the place from where the suspected hoax call was made to the citys Police Control Room (PCR) on February 16. ||||

An anonymous phone caller has threatened to kill Bollywood superstar Salman Khan, prompting the Mumbai Police to launch a probe, an official said today.
The phone call was made to the city’s Police Control Room on February 16, police said. Police suspected it was a hoax call and launched a probe though no case has been filed yet.
Police has managed to locate the place from where the call was made.
Salman Khan has recently delivered two back-to-back hits with ‘Prem Ratan Dhan Payo’ and ‘Bajrangi Bhaijaan.’ The actor is currently working on his next film Sultan.
