Limited edition gold-coloured Nexus 6P launched ||||
 Huawei has launched a limited edition gold colour variant of its Google Nexus 6P (64GB storage) smartphone in India. The special edition device will be exclusively available on e-commerce website Flipkart for 43,999 starting Monday. The smartphone, that was launched in the country in late September, was previously available in silver and grey colours. ||||

Huawei has announced that its limited edition gold version of Nexus 6P (64 GB) will now be exclusively available on Flipkart at Rs 43,999. The gold edition of the Nexus 6P will go on sale today.

“The Huawei Nexus 6P has been well received by the Indian audience and keeping in mind the niche target group, we have introduced the gold colour Special Edition of the smartphone,” said Allen Wang, President, Huawei India – Consumer Business Group said.

Huawei Nexus 6P comes with a 5.7-inch screen with WQHD (2560×1440) AMOLED display at 518 ppi and Corning Gorilla Glass 4. It is powered by an Octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 v2.1 SoC supported by 3GB of RAM. The device features 12.3MP rear camera with IR laser-assisted autofocus.
Running on Google’s latest version of Android, Marshmallow, Nexus 6P was launched at a price Rs 39,999 (32GB) and Rs 42,999 (64 GB) with two colors Aluminum (Silver) and Graphite (Black) in India.
