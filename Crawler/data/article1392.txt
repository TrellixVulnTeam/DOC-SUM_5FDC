Jitu Rai wins silver medal at Shooting World Cup ||||
 Shooter Jitu Rai clinched the silver medal in 10-metre air pistol event at the International Shooting Sport Federation World Cup in Baku, Azerbaijan, on Saturday. This is Rai's sixth World Cup medal, and his second this year, after a gold medal in Bangkok in March. In 2014, Rai had become the first Indian shooter to qualify for the Rio Olympics. ||||

Jitu Rai gave a glimpse of his preparations for Rio Olympics as he claimed a silver medal in the men’s 10m air pistol event at the ISSF world cup in Baku.
Jitu shot a brilliant 199.5 in the final and finished second only behind Felipe Almeida Wu.
He qualified for the final at sixth position in qualification with a score of 580-22X.
This is Jitu’s sixth medal the World Cup, all of which he has achieved in the last three years.
