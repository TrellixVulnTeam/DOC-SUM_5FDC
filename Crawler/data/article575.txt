Shahid as Deepika's husband in Padmavati: Report ||||
 As per reports, Sanjay Leela Bhansali is considering actor Shahid Kapoor for the role of Deepika Padukone's husband in his biopic on Rani Padmavati. Earlier, Vicky Kaushal reportedly backed out from the role. However, it is being said that Deepika refused to work with Kaushal or any TV actor as she wanted only a "big star" owing to intimate scenes. ||||

If rumours are to be believed, then very soon, Shahid Kapoor will be seen for the first time in a Sanjay Leela Bhansali film and that too as Deepika Padukone's husband.

According to a report in SpotBoyE.com, a source told the website, "Bhansali wants Shahid to play the Chittor King who is married to Rani Padmini of Chittorgarh."
Earlier it was reported that Vicky Kaushal might have a shot at playing Raja Rawal Ratan Singh of Mewar in Sanjay Leela Bhansali's ambitious biopic of Rani Padmavati, the queen of Mewar.
In fact, a source was d telling PTI, "He has auditioned for the role (of Deepika Padukone's husband). He is in talks but nothing is finalised yet."
If Shahid Kapoor does get to star in Padmavati, the film will be one star-studded affair with Ranveer Singh already slated to play the male lead in the film. Padmavati will feature the return of lovebirds Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone for the third time onscreen after Bhansali's Goliyon Ki Rasleela Ram-Leela (2013) and Bajirao Mastani (2015).
