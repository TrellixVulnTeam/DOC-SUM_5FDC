Katrina's look from 'Baar Baar Dekho' revealed ||||
 Katrina Kaif's look from her upcoming film 'Baar Baar Dekho' has been revealed. She can be seen sporting a chopped hairdo in the pictures that have surfaced on social media. As per reports, the actress has cut her hair that short to give her character a realistic feel. The directorial debut of Nitya Mehra also features Sidharth Malhotra and Tabu. ||||

Bollywood actress Katrina Kaif, who is currently shooting for her upcoming film ‘Baar Baar Dekho’, will be seen in a new avatar in Nitya Mehra’s directorial. The actress will be sporting a short hairdo for the film.
Katrina Kaif, who likes to experiment with her looks, has chopped her hair to give authenticity for her character.
This is not the first time that the actress has transformed herself for her character in a film. Earlier Katrina was seen in red hair for her upcoming film ‘Fitoor’ which grabbed many eyeballs. Directed by Abhishek Kapoor, ‘Fitoor’ will bring Adiya Roy Kapur and Katrina on silver screen for the first time.
‘Baar Baar Dekho’, which marks Nitya Mehra’s debut in direction, will see Katrina opposite Sidharth Malhotra and Tabu in lead roles.
Katrina’s looks will go through various transformations and she will also use prosthetics for getting a certain look for the film.
Produced by Ritesh Sidhwani, Karan Johar and Farhan Akhtar, the film is scheduled for release in 2016.
